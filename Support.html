Getting Help

Email: synapse-devworks@gmail.com
Response Time: We typically respond within 24-48 hours

When Contacting Support

To help us assist you more efficiently, please include:

  1. Device Information: Your iPhone/iPad model and iOS version
  2. App Version: You can find this in Settings > About within the app
  3. Description of Issue: What you were trying to do and what happened
  4. Screenshots: If applicable, screenshots help us understand the issue

Common Issues

Sync Issues

Make sure you're connected to the internet and try force-closing and reopening the app.

Transaction Problems

Check that all required fields are filled out correctly when adding transactions.

Budget Calculations

Verify that your budget items are set up with the correct amounts and categories.

Feature Requests

We love hearing from our users! If you have ideas for new features or improvements, please send them our way. While we can't implement every suggestion, we carefully consider all feedback for future updates.

Privacy & Data

Your financial data is stored locally on your device and is never transmitted to our servers without your explicit consent. For more information, please review our Privacy Policy.
